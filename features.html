OpenCHS Features

This is a current list of features that are available in OpenCHS. We update the system frequently. A detailed roadmap of OpenCHS is available here.

Alternatively, download the Android app from the playstore and try it out yourself.

Platform features for field workers (android device)

  • Individual search and registration
  • Enrolment (and exit) of individuals into health programs
  • Capture visit details for for individuals in the program
  • Dashboards for health worker to manage their upcoming, due and missed visits
  • Automated generation of visit schedule on enrolment and visit completion
  • Smart forms for enrolment, registration and visits - based on individual's health record
  • View complete health record of an individual
  • Support for visualising individual health information in form of graphs

Health modules for field worker based on the platform

Mother reproductive health

  • ANC, Delivery and PNC visit schedule and data management
  • Automatic high risk identification
  • Automatic investigation advice recommendation and referral

Child growth and immunisation

  • Growth information tracking
  • Immunisation schedule management
  • WHO growth charts (height, weight and age based. Z-scores, Nutritional status)

Outpatient

  • Support for medication for 16 minor ailments

Reporting and dashboard platform

  • Dashboard specific to health programs
  • Support for drill-down, charts, progress indicator, pivot tables

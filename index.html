OpenCHS - Open Source Field Worker Platform

OpenCHS is a platform that empowers field workers who provide services to their customers in a field setup and also do data collection. OpenCHS provides an android application that can be used in the field without Internet connectivity and web browser based application for program coordinators/managers who support and supervise the field workers. OpenCHS has been implemented the most in public health but it is also implemented for other sectors.
